SOCIAL WORK CONFERENCE -

CALL FOR PAPERS:

Abstracts are invited on the above theme and sub-themes by researchers, academicians, policy makers and analysts, PhD students and specialists in the area of Human Security and Human rights. The official language of the conference will be English without any official translations for the plenary, panel and poster presentations.

AUTHOR GUIDELINES:

Abstracts: Abstracts to be submitted in English as an email attachment word document by February 28, 2017. The acknowledgement of selected abstracts will be made by April 15th, 2017. Selected papers from the conference will be published. Abstracts should include the following details:

  • MS Word or Times Roman, 12 point font
  • Author’s name or names, address or addresses, telephone number and e-mail address
  • Title
  • Oral or poster presentation
  • Audio-visual equipment (indicate if needed)
  • Abstract should not exceed 300 words

COMPLETED PAPERS:

Completed papers for the conference must be received by August 15th , 2017. Please, note that the papers should be in Microsoft Word for Windows; Times New Roman 12 points with left margin and right margin 3 Centimetres and 2.5 Centimetres respectively. The paper should be double spaced.

About Us

Department of Social Work, University of Botswana offers professional programmes in social work: Diploma, Bachelor’s, Master’s and Doctoral degrees. These programmes are constantly reviewed to ensure relevance.

The Department has a vibrant, innovative and dedicated staff compliment of 17 members specialised in various fields of Social work including clinical, supervision, social policy, social work education, and social services.
